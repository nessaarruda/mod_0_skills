class Protein

Attributes

type (string)
weight (float)
cooked (string)
is_it_vegan (boolean)

Methods

add_protein (modifies attribute `type` by adding a new protein)
check_weight (modifies attribute `weight` by checking the weight of the chosen protein)
add_cooking_instruction (modifies attribute `cooked` by printing rare, medium, well done)
check_vegan (modifies attribute is_it_vegan by printing "yes" or "no" depending on the boolean)
